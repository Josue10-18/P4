My Testimony & Mission

My name is Josue Ortiz. I was raised in the Mormon church and went faithfully until about age 13, when I slowly drifted away from the faith. As I grew older, I fell into a five-year struggle with lust addiction, from late 2019 to early 2025.

But through the grace of God and by finally understanding the true gospel of Jesus Christ, I was saved, renewed, and completely freed from that addiction. God gave me a new heart—a heart that longs for Jesus, for holiness, and for bringing others to Him.

Today, I truly understand what it means to follow Jesus—not as a religion, but as a relationship. I’m now walking a new journey as a non-denominational follower of Christ, pursuing Him daily and growing in His Word.

God has given me a passion for evangelism, and I share the gospel in creative, authentic ways—both in everyday conversations and through social media.

You can find me on Instagram and TikTok @fzdxchrist, where I use my platform to point people to Christ and share His transforming power.
